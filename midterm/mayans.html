Overview of The Maya

The Maya civilization was known for its advanced writing system, mathematical knowledge, and architectural marvels. They thrived in Mesoamerica and left behind remarkable cultural legacies.

Key Contributions

  • Creation of the Maya calendar
  • Advancements in mathematics, including the concept of zero
  • Construction of elaborate pyramids and cities

Important Figures

  • Pakal the Great - King of Palenque
  • Lady Xoc - Queen of Yaxchilan
  • Yax Kuk Mo - Founder of the Copan dynasty
