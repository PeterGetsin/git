Overview of Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egypt is one of the world's oldest civilizations, known for its monumental architecture, advanced knowledge in mathematics and astronomy, and remarkable contributions to art and literature.

Key Contributions

  • Construction of the Pyramids
  • Development of hieroglyphics
  • Advancements in medicine and engineering

Important Figures

  • Pharaoh Ramses II - Known for military campaigns and monumental structures
  • Cleopatra VII - The last pharaoh of Egypt
  • Imhotep - Architect of the Step Pyramid and early physician
